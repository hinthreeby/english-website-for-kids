### **Trang 1: The Beginning**

You are walking in the park. Suddenly, you see a small, white puppy under a tree. He looks sad and lost.


- **Option A:** Give the puppy some of your sandwich. (**Go to Page 2**)
- **Option B:** Look at his collar to find his name. (**Go to Page 3**)

---

### **Trang 2: The Hungry Puppy**

The puppy eats the sandwich. He is very happy now! He wags his tail. But wait, he starts running towards the forest!


- **Option A:** Follow him into the forest. (**Go to Page 4**)
- **Option B:** Stay in the park and call for help. (**Go to Page 5**)

---

### **Trang 3: The Secret Name**

You look at the collar. It says: *"My name is Snowy. I live at Blue House."* You look around and see a blue house far away.

- **Option A:** Walk to the Blue House. (**Go to Page 6**)
- **Option B:** Wait for Snowy’s owner in the park. (**Go to Page 5**)

---

### **Trang 4: The Forest Adventure**


The forest is beautiful but dark. Snowy stops at a big river. There is a bridge and a small boat.

- **Option A:** Cross the bridge. (**Go to Page 7**)
- **Option B:** Use the boat. (**Go to Page 8**)

---

### **Trang 5: Waiting in the Park (Ending 1)**


**Ending: The Safe Return.**
An old lady comes to the park. She is looking for her dog. "Snowy!" she shouts. Snowy jumps into her arms. The lady says "Thank you!" and gives you a gold medal for being a kind child.

- *Vocabulary learned: Lost, Found, Kind.*

---

### **Trang 6: The Blue House (Ending 2)**

**Ending: New Friends.**


You arrive at the Blue House. A little boy opens the door. He is so happy to see Snowy! He invites you inside for cookies and milk. You have a new friend now!

- *Vocabulary learned: Neighbor, Happy, Cookies.*

---

### **Trang 7: The Magic Bridge (Ending 3)**

**Ending: The Fairy Party.**

The bridge is magical! It leads to a secret garden. There is a party with many animals. Snowy was actually a magic dog! You and Snowy dance all day.

- *Vocabulary learned: Magic, Bridge, Dance.*

---

### **Trang 8: The Boat Trip (Ending 4)**


**Ending: The River Journey.**
The boat floats gently down the river. You see ducks and colorful fish. The boat stops at your own backyard! Your mom is waiting with a big hug. What an adventure!

- *Vocabulary learned: Boat, River, Family.*
